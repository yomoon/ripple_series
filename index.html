Scenario

daily life, from morning to night

MORNING

To start a day, LIGHT will turn on automatically to wake you up in the morning.

PLUG already turn on the connected electric kettle to boil water for you to make coffee.

LEAVE

Once you leave home, PLUG turn off connected appliances to save power for reducing your bill payment.

SHOCK detects shock and glass breakage, you’ll get notification if someone is trying to break window.

SMOKE detects smoke and heat, it sends alarm and also notification.

HOME

DOOR monitor your home entrance, it sends you notification when door is open or you can check status on APP anytime.

When door is open, DOOR triggers LIGHT or PLUG to turn on, your hallway is bright to welcome you home.

GOOD NIGHT

When it’s time to bed, PLUG will turn off connected appliance and LIGHT will dim automatically, just set the schedule in APP.

If it gets hot, WEATHER will tell PLUG to turn on the connected fan, you don’t need to wake up in the middle of night.
